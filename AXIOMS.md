# openIronSDA Axioms: The Unbreakable Laws

## ⚖️ Axiom I: Zero Source Logic
This repository shall contain only **Intent (Specifications)** and **Lattices (Diagrams/Nodes)**. Direct compiled source code for the DCS is strictly prohibited to maintain the separation of Spec and Sovereign implementation.

## ⚖️ Axiom II: Sovereign Execution (The Ghost)
All tools used to verify or synthesize intent must live in a local-only `tools/` directory. No proprietary implementation logic shall be pushed to the public substrate.

## ⚖️ Axiom III: Causal Proof Obligation
No change shall be merged into `main` without a **Rank Ω Proof** generated by the **8-Axiom Convergence Cycle**. Human approval is a secondary attestation; Tool-based verification is the primary gate.

## ⚖️ Axiom IV: Traceability of Failure
Every anomaly detected must be mapped back to its causal ancestor via **CCA (Causal Convergence Analysis)** and documented in the **Edge Case Vault**.

## ⚖️ Axiom V: The Vibe Pillar
Success is measured not by uptime alone, but by the **Aesthetic of Success**: Feel (Peace), Taste (Integrity), and Look (Clarity).
